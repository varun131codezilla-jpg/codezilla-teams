The Fine Print — Made Plain

We know the things that actually bite are never the headline fee. Here are the exact commercial terms that other EOR providers bury in their contracts.

Term & Notice Periods

  • Minimum Commitment: 3 months initial engagement per employee, flexible month-to-month thereafter.
  • Notice Period: 30 days written notice for contract termination or employee offboarding.
  • Early Exit: If you terminate within the first 3 months, the remaining months' management fees apply. After that, month-to-month with 30-day notice.

FX & Currency Policy

  • Exchange Rate: Live Reuters/Bloomberg interbank mid-market rate, recalculated on the 1st of each billing month.
  • FX Fee: A single, fully disclosed 0.75% currency processing fee — no hidden spread or arbitrage.
  • Billing Currency: Choose USD, GBP, or EUR. One itemized monthly invoice covering everything.

Exit & Transition Fees

  • Transition Fee: Guaranteed $0 / £0 / €0. No buyout penalty when you move employees to your own Indian entity.
  • Non-Solicitation: Zero restrictions. You can hire your team directly at any time.
  • Severance: Exact statutory severance calculated under state labor rules, passed through at cost with zero markup.
