Payroll, Taxes & FX Transparency

We calculate employee salaries in Indian Rupees (INR) and bill your organization in USD, GBP, or EUR based on live Reuters/Bloomberg interbank mid-market exchange rates. We charge a single, transparent fixed 0.75% currency processing fee with zero hidden FX spreads or markups.

We calculate the employee's monthly salary in Indian Rupees (INR), deduct mandatory Tax Deducted at Source (TDS) according to Indian Income Tax slabs, deposit Provident Fund (EPFO) and ESIC contributions, and disburse net salary directly to their Indian bank account on the last working day of the month.

We issue a single consolidated monthly invoice in your preferred home currency (USD, GBP, or EUR). The invoice clearly details employee gross salaries, exact pass-through statutory contributions, and our transparent flat monthly management fee.

Employees receive statutory benefits including Provident Fund (EPFO), ESIC (where applicable under salary caps), statutory bonus, and gratuity tracking. Additionally, we enroll your team into tier-1 group medical insurance covering the employee and their immediate family members.
